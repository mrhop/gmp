ABOUT

Tucher group is the holding company of the businesses of the Tucher Family. The Tucher are an old and traditional German family and were first mentioned in 1309. During the 14th and 15th century the Tucher Family made its fortune by trading from the central city of Nuremberg to the rest of Europe, especially Venice, which was the European end of the silk road at that time.

Now Tucher Group is leading in the fields of project development, investment and engineering, with a clear focus on Europe and China as well as the countries along the New Silk Road under “One Belt, One Road”. And its subsidiary group ESI has been implementing real estate developments in Asia including the “German Residence” a luxury apartment project in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia.

The project brings the best of German expertise in the real estate sector and presenting you the “authentic German” property which underlines VALUE, QUALITY and COMFORT with German engineering, high quality brand utilities and European interior design.
